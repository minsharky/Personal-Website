about me

resume

Hello! My name is Minseo Kim and I like to build cool things that people can use and enjoy. I am a student at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Psychology working to be a Software Engineer.

As a CS and Psych double major, I love looking at the intersection of how people interact with tech and vice-versa. Being able to then utilize those observations in order to create tools and platforms that can shape the way people see, use, and appreciate tech is an incredible feat I wish to be a part of.

I have helped create numerous projects such as an online board game platform web application and a handful of recreated retro games such as Tetris, PacMan, and Minesweeper. More recently, I've been using the Unity game engine to build my own 2D and 3D games and am excited to share them with you all.

When I am not working, I love to exercise my creative energy through music, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and playing cool video games.

I am proficient in JavaScript, Java, C, C++, and C#. I also have experience using HTML, CSS, React.js, Git, Unity, and LaTex.
